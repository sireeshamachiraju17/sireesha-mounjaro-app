Twilio quick-setup guide
Fill your .env file on the server

TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN=your_token
TWILIO_WHATSAPP_FROM=whatsapp:+14155238886
RECIPIENT_PHONE=+919945977880

3. Send "join <your-code>" to +14155238886 on WhatsApp from your phone

4. Copy your Account SID and Auth Token into .env on your server

5. Restart the server — reminders fire automatically every Sunday 8am IST!

How Mounjaro works for you

Dual-action mechanism — Tirzepatide activates both GIP and GLP-1 receptors simultaneously, making it more powerful than single-receptor medications.

What to expect — Appetite naturally reduces, blood sugar stabilises, and your body preferentially burns stored fat. Clinical trials showed 15–22% body weight reduction over 24 weeks.

Dose escalation — Starting at 2.5mg and stepping up every 4 weeks lets your body adapt, minimising side effects while maximising results.
